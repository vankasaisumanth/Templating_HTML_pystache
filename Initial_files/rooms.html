ROOMS

The suites at Octave Suites are lavish, spacious, and elaborately built for discerning travelers and their families. Every suite has a king-size bed and is large enough to accommodate extra beds along with all luxuries expected from a modern boutique hotel.

There are two categories of suites at Octave Suites: Deluxe Suites & Executive Suites

Deluxe Suites

Deluxe Suites are the most popular suite category at Octave Suites. The suites are large and measuring 350 sq ft with lavish interiors. Following are some of the key features

  • King-Size Bed (for 3 PAX)
  • LED Television
  • Sofa Sets & Coffee Table
  • Minibar
  • Cable Television
  • Electronic Safe
  • Tea/Coffee Maker
  • Study Table
  • High Speed Wi-Fi
  • Weighing Scale
  • Mineral Water
  • Bathroom Amenities

Executive Suites

Executive Suites are premium suites suitable for the discerning traveler with that extra sense of style. The suites are large and measuring 450 sq ft with lavish interiors. Following are some of the key features:

  • King-Size Bed (for 3 PAX)
  • LED Television
  • 2 Sofa Sets & Coffee Table
  • Minibar
  • Cable Television
  • Electronic Safe
  • Tea/Coffee Maker
  • Study Table
  • High Speed Wi-Fi
  • Bathrobe
  • Weighing Scale
  • Mineral Water
  • Bathroom Amenities
